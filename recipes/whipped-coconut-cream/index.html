Whipped Coconut Cream

Servings: 4 to 6 (Makes 1 cup)

Time: 10 minutes, plus 24 hours chilling

Ingredients

  • 2 (14-ounce) cans coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Equipment

  • Measuring Spoons
  • Stand Mixers (High-End)

Recipe Notes

  1. To ensure that the cream whips properly, be sure to use full-fat coconut milk here.

Instructions

  1. Refrigerate unopened cans of coconut milk for at least 24 hours to ensure 2 distinct layers form. Skim top layer of coconut cream from each can and measure out 1 cup (save any extra cream and milky liquid for another use). Using stand mixer fitted with whisk attachment, whip coconut cream, sugar, and vanilla on low speed until well combined, about 30 seconds. Increase speed to high and whip until mixture thickens and soft peaks form, about 2 minutes. (Whipped coconut cream can be refrigerated for up to 4 days.)
