Arnold Palmer

Servings: Makes 1 drink

Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup iced tea
  • ⅓ cup Lemonade Concentrate
  • ⅓ cup water

Equipment

  • Barspoons
  • Large Liquid Measuring Cups

Recipe Notes

  1. Use either black or green iced tea, whichever you prefer.

Instructions

  1. Combine ice, iced tea, lemonade concentrate, and water in tall glass and stir gently to combine. Serve.
